Dead Lines

Privacy Policy

The only data collected by the developer of Dead Lines are the individual user’s email address that they use to contact the developer from the Sounds and Communication table within the application. These email addresses are used to respond to questions and inquiries that the users of the application sends to the developer.

The developer of this application, Dead Lines, does not collect any usage data. All usage data is stored on the user’s device.

Data is transferred when making in-app purchases solely to Apple’s App Store. Account data is not collected by the developer of Dead Lines.
